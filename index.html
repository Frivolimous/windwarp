Import a BITMAP FILE to load your own level!


1. Each Pixel = 1 tile (40 pixels)
2. The following colors are acceptable:
'255,0,0': 'player' (need 1 of these)
'0,255,0': 'normal ground'
'255,255,0': 'spring'
'255,170,0': 'exploding'
'255,255,255': 'goal'
'0,0,0': 'ghost'
'0,255,255': 'checkpoint'
Everything else is SKY

How to Play

Win the level by stepping on a white block. Get there as a fast as you can!

Use w,a,s,d or arrow keys to move around
CTRL also rolls and SPACE also jumps
"V" uses your infinite jetpack (on some levels it's cheating)

Use advanced movement controls: Rolling, Climbing, Wall Jumps and Diving!
